Chicken

Chicken is a type of poultry that is widely consumed around the world.
It is known for its versatility in cooking and can be prepared in various ways, including grilling, frying, baking, and roasting.
Chicken is a good source of protein and can be used in a variety of dishes, from salads to soups to main courses.

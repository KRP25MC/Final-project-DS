Classic Smashed Cheeseburger

Enjoy a juicy and flavorful classic smashed cheeseburger with crispy edges and melted cheese. Perfect for burger lovers!

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground beef (80% lean)
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 4 slices American cheese
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • Butter, for toasting buns
  • Optional toppings: lettuce, tomato, pickles, ketchup, mustard, mayonnaise

Instructions:

  1. Divide the ground beef into 4 equal portions and gently shape into loose balls. Do not overwork the meat.
  2. Heat a cast-iron skillet or griddle over medium-high heat until very hot.
  3. Place the beef balls onto the skillet and smash them flat with a spatula. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Cook for 2-3 minutes until the edges are crispy, then flip and place a slice of cheese on top of each patty.
  5. Cook for another 1-2 minutes until the cheese is melted and the burger is cooked through.
  6. Butter the hamburger buns and toast them in the skillet until golden brown.
  7. Assemble the burgers by placing the patties on the buns and adding your favorite toppings.
  8. Serve immediately and enjoy!

Cooking Tips:

  • Use a heavy spatula to smash the burgers for the best crispy edges.
  • Do not press down on the burgers after flipping to retain their juiciness.
  • Customize your toppings to suit your taste preferences.

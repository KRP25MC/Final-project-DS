Easy Pepperoni Pizza

Make a delicious and easy pepperoni pizza at home with this quick recipe. Perfect for a family dinner or a casual gathering!

Ingredients:

  • 1 can (15 oz) Hunt's® Tomato Sauce
  • 1 pre-made pizza crust
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 package (3.5 oz) sliced pepperoni
  • 1/2 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. Place the pizza crust on a baking sheet or pizza stone.
  3. Spread the Hunt's® Tomato Sauce evenly over the crust.
  4. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
  5. Top with sliced pepperoni, arranging evenly across the pizza.
  6. Sprinkle oregano and garlic powder over the top.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  8. Remove from the oven, let cool slightly, slice, and serve.

Cooking Tips:

  • For a crispier crust, bake the pizza directly on a pizza stone or oven rack.
  • Add your favorite toppings like mushrooms, bell peppers, or olives for variety.
  • Use fresh mozzarella for a creamier texture.

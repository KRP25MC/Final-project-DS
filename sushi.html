Simple Sushi with Avocado and Salmon

Learn how to make simple sushi rolls with fresh salmon and creamy avocado.
Perfect for beginners and a delicious treat!

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup sushi rice
  • 2 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1/2 tsp sugar
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 4 sheets of nori (seaweed)
  • 100g fresh salmon, thinly sliced
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • Soy sauce (for serving)
  • Wasabi and pickled ginger (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Rinse the sushi rice under cold water until
    the water runs clear. Cook the rice according to the package instructions.
  2. Mix the rice vinegar, sugar, and salt in a small bowl.
    Gently fold the mixture into the cooked rice and let it cool.
  3. Place a sheet of nori on a bamboo sushi mat, shiny side down.
  4. Spread a thin layer of rice evenly over the
    nori, leaving a 1-inch border at the top.
  5. Place slices of salmon and avocado in a line
    across the center of the rice.
  6. Using the bamboo mat, roll the sushi tightly from
    the bottom edge, pressing gently to seal.
  7. Slice the roll into bite-sized pieces using a sharp knife.
  8. Serve with soy sauce, wasabi, and pickled ginger if desired.

Cooking Tips:

  • Use a sharp knife to slice the sushi rolls cleanly.
  • Keep a bowl of water nearby to wet your hands and prevent the rice from sticking.
  • Experiment with other fillings like cucumber or crab sticks for variety.

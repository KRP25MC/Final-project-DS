Parmentier Potatoes

Serve parmentier potatoes instead of chips or roasties for a more elegant side.
A classic dish flavoured with rosemary and garlic that's easy to make.

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg potatoes, peeled and diced
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tbsp fresh rosemary, chopped
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 200°C (400°F).
  2. Boil the diced potatoes in salted water for 5 minutes, then drain well.
  3. In a large bowl, toss the potatoes with olive oil, minced garlic, rosemary, salt, and pepper.
  4. Spread the potatoes evenly on a baking tray lined with parchment paper.
  5. Roast in the oven for 30-40 minutes, turning occasionally, until golden and crispy.
  6. Serve hot as a side dish.

Cooking Tips:

  • For extra crispiness, let the potatoes dry completely after boiling before roasting.
  • Use fresh rosemary for the best flavor.
  • Pair with roasted meats or grilled vegetables for a complete meal.

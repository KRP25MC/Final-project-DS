Classic Lasagna

Enjoy a hearty and delicious classic lasagna with layers of rich meat sauce,
creamy béchamel, and melted cheese. Perfect for family dinners!

Ingredients:

  • 12 lasagna sheets
  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 400g canned tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 cups béchamel sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Cook the lasagna sheets according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef over medium heat until browned.
    Add the onion and garlic, and sauté until softened.
  4. Stir in the canned tomatoes, tomato paste, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 15 minutes.
  5. In a baking dish, spread a thin layer of meat sauce. Add a layer of lasagna sheets,
    followed by béchamel sauce, and a sprinkle of mozzarella cheese.
    Repeat the layers until all ingredients are used, finishing with a layer of béchamel and cheese on top.
  6. Sprinkle Parmesan cheese over the top layer.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 30-40 minutes, or until the top is golden and bubbly.
  8. Let the lasagna rest for 10 minutes before serving.

Cooking Tips:

  • Use fresh herbs for a more vibrant flavor.
  • Let the lasagna rest before cutting to ensure clean slices.
  • Pair with a fresh green salad and garlic bread for a complete meal.
